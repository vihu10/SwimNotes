Features

📝

Fast note-taking

Log feedback per event in seconds — even messy shorthand. Voice input and tap-to-add examples help you capture it before you forget.

🤖

AI-ready prompts

One tap builds a ready-to-paste prompt and opens ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini with it filled in. No API keys, no cost.

📅

Coach reminders

Generate a calendar reminder to follow up at practice — auto-timed from your uploaded schedule, no calendar account needed.

📋

Meet history

Revisit past meets and let AI surface the feedback patterns that keep repeating across the season.

🔒

Private by design

Everything stays in your browser. No accounts, no servers, no tracking — your notes never leave your device.

🌗

Great on any device

A responsive light/dark design that feels at home on your phone at the meet, or your laptop afterward.

How it works

  1. 1

    Log feedback at the meet

    Add a meet, then jot what your coach says after each event.

  2. 2

    Generate a prompt

    Tap Summarize to turn your raw notes into an AI-ready prompt.

  3. 3

    Get your summary

    Open a free AI tool with the prompt prefilled, then paste the organized summary back into SwimNotes.

  4. 4

    Follow up with your coach

    Add a calendar reminder so you ask the right questions at your next practice.

🔒

Your data stays yours

SwimNotes is a static web app with no backend. Your meets, notes, and summaries are saved only in this browser's local storage, on this device. They're never uploaded, and there are no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking.

The app never calls an AI itself — you choose which AI tool to use and exactly what to paste into it. Because nothing is collected by any operator, regulations like COPPA, FERPA, and CCPA aren't triggered.

  • No server — static site only
  • No login or personal data collected
  • No AI API calls or keys baked into the app
  • Calendar reminders are a local file download — no calendar access
